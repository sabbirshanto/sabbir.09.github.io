About Me

This is Md. Sabbir Al Ahsan, a dedicated Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at East Delta University, Chattogram. My academic journey includes pursuing an M.Sc. in CSE at Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), where my ongoing thesis explores a novel framework for hotel recommendation systems through multimodal analysis.

My core research interests lie at the intersection of **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Big Data Analytics, and Natural Language Processing**. I am passionately committed to advancing these fields and am actively seeking challenging PhD opportunities in Computer Science in the USA to expand my research horizons and contribute meaningfully to the academic community.
